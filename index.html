Difficult to imagine by looking outside, but Fall is right around the corner! We have enjoyed some of the best weather here in the Pacific Northwest! Not all of us are used to it, but beautiful all the same. Now that fall is almost here, it is time for the Everett Fall Home Show! We will be sampling our fresh roasted coffee on site, so please join us for some fun and some great shopping opportunities. With fall comes the holiday season on it's heels! Get ready early!
